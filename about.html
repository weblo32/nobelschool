Our Heritage & Vision

Noble School & Junior College was established in 2010 with a vision to provide standard secondary and higher secondary education in Thane.

Our 25-acre lush green campus in Shahapur provides a serene atmosphere for study. Over the years, we have built standard science labs, computer rooms, and libraries containing reference guides, sample board exam question sets, and competitive entrance books.

We are committed to providing personalized guidance to every student, helping them build solid conceptual foundations in Science, Commerce, and Arts, preparing them for higher studies and board success.

Key Credentials

  • ✅ Affiliated with Maharashtra State Board (MSBSHSE), Pune
  • ✅ Recognized by DYDE, Government of Maharashtra
  • ✅ Integrated JEE, NEET & CA coaching cell
  • ✅ Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry & Biology Labs
  • ✅ ISO 9001:2015 Certified School Infrastructure

From the Principal's Desk

Welcome to the academic year at Noble Junior College. Our junior college has achieved 100% success rate in board exams. We place special focus on regular attendance, monthly class tests, and comprehensive board writing practices.

For Science students, we offer integrated classes for JEE, NEET, and MHT-CET, ensuring they don't have to seek outside coaching. Commerce students are guided by CA faculty, preparing them for the CA Foundation early on.

Quality Policy Statement

"We commit to provide quality education by maintaining disciplined attendance patterns, conducting systematic weekly evaluations, and training our faculty on the revised HSC board syllabus."

Every department is audited regularly to check that course milestones are completed on time, board practical sheets are verified, and student reviews are logged for action.
