Admission Eligibility Criteria

Science Stream (FYJC)

  • Must have passed Class 10 (SSC) Board exam or equivalent (CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE) with Science and Mathematics.
  • Admission is conducted via the Online Centralised Admission Process (CAP Portal).
  • Bifocal subjects (Computer Science & Electronics) are allocated purely on merit lists.

Commerce Stream (FYJC)

  • Must have passed Class 10 (SSC) Board exam or equivalent from recognized national or state boards.
  • Allocation is based on Class 10 aggregate scores and CAP portal choice allocation.
  • Information Technology (IT) is offered on a merit basis in place of second languages.

Arts Stream (FYJC)

  • Must have passed Class 10 (SSC) Board exam or equivalent from recognized boards.
  • No stream-specific prerequisites. Direct admission based on CAP list choice allocation.
  • Psychology and Political Science have seat limits and are allocated on request merit.
