Co-Curricular & Student Clubs

Education is complete when students participate in group activities, discussions, and creative labs. Our clubs help teenagers explore interests outside the board syllabus.

Students can choose to join any club at the beginning of Class 11. Each club has a dedicated faculty supervisor who schedules weekly sessions, seminars, and events.

Participating in these activities builds confidence, teamwork skills, and helps students create portfolios for higher university admissions.

Why Join Clubs?

Explore practical science, public debate, nature trekking, and team sports.

Active Student Clubs

Junior Science Club

Schedules science workshops, robotics exhibitions, and visits to laboratories. Helps students build working physics and chemistry models.

🗣️

Literary & Debate Club

Hosts inter-class speech debates, spelling contests, and creative essay writing sessions to build English and communication skills.

🌱

Nature & Eco Club

Organizes tree plantations, environmental awareness drives, recycling campaigns, and nature walks in nearby forest areas.

NSS & Sports Council

Developing discipline, social concern, and athletic ability in our students.

National Service Scheme (NSS) Unit

Our registered NSS unit encourages students to participate in community service. Activities include cleanliness campaigns in nearby villages, blood donation drives, and public safety rallies.

  • 🌿 Organizing Swachh Bharat Abhiyan cleaning weeks.
  • 🌿 Medical checkup camps for local residents.
  • 🌿 Arranging digital literacy classes for rural children.

Sports Council

Noble School provides standard facilities for indoor and outdoor sports. The Sports Council schedules the annual sports meet, inter-house matches, and trains athletes for state board sports tournaments.

  • 🏆 Outdoor: Cricket, Football, Volleyball, Kabaddi.
  • 🏆 Indoor: Carrom, Chess, Table Tennis.
  • 🏆 Dedicated coaches for track athletics and sprint events.
