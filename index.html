Welcome to Noble School & Junior College

Providing standard secondary and higher secondary education for over 15 years, Noble School & Junior College is a premier hub of learning in Thane.

Affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board (MSBSHSE), Pune, we offer a safe, modern, and green academic environment. We provide standard classrooms, equipped chemistry, physics, and biology labs, computer IT rooms, and a library with thousands of student reference books.

Our integrated curriculum provides regular lecture sessions, weekly tests, and detailed training for board exams as well as crucial entrance exams like JEE, NEET, MHT-CET, and CA Foundation.
